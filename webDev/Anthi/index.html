About Us

We’re the quaint Cheers-like spot in Pinellas Park where everyone knows your name, and we’re ready to share our simple, home-style cooking with you. Serving classic diner breakfasts all day long, plus big, delicious burgers, steaks, and sandwiches, our hearty portions and warm, inviting atmosphere will have you feeling right at home. We like to show a little sweet flair with our fare too, which is why we serve to-die-for cakes, pies, and other classic desserts. Taste the love in our food, and join us at Tampa Bay’s friendliest diner today.
